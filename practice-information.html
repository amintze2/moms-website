Practice Information

A sensible approach to nutrition for lasting results

About the Practice

Dawn Saks is a nutritionist who believes in a sensible approach to diet. Rather than advocating quick fixes or fad diets, Dawn helps patients make sustainable changes that support long-term health. She works closely with physicians and their patients, offering expert nutrition guidance tailored to each individual's medical history, lifestyle, and goals.

Areas of Practice

  • Weight loss and management
  • Gastrointestinal disorders
  • Cholesterol and triglyceride reduction
  • Eating disorders and emotional eating
  • Wellness and diabetes management
  • Hypertension treatment
  • Pregnancy and pediatric nutrition
