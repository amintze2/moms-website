What We Treat

Practice Information

A sensible, evidence-based approach to nutrition for a wide range of conditions and health goals.

Our Approach

Nutrition that fits your life

Dawn Saks believes in a sensible, practical approach to diet — one that works with your real life, not against it. She collaborates closely with your physicians to ensure nutrition is integrated with your overall care.

Specializations

Areas of Practice

Weight Loss & Management

Sustainable weight management through personalized meal planning and behavioral strategies — no crash diets, just lasting change.

Cholesterol & Triglyceride Reduction

Dietary interventions to lower cholesterol and triglyceride levels and reduce cardiovascular risk.

Wellness & Diabetes Management

Specialized counseling for pre-diabetes, Type 1, Type 2, and gestational diabetes. Certified Diabetes Educator on staff.

Gastrointestinal Disorders

Nutritional support for IBS, IBD, GERD, celiac disease, and other GI conditions.

Eating Disorders & Emotional Eating

Compassionate, non-judgmental support for patients working through disordered eating and emotional relationships with food.

Hypertension Treatment

Dietary strategies to lower blood pressure and reduce reliance on medication through evidence-based nutrition plans.

Pregnancy & Pediatric Nutrition

Specialized nutritional guidance for pregnancy, postpartum recovery, and children's health and development.
