About Dawn

20 years helping patients eat better and live well

Dawn Saks, MS, RDN, CDE is a Registered Dietitian accredited by the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics and a Certified Diabetes Educator. She holds a Master's degree in clinical nutrition from NYU, graduating with honors.

Dawn consults for physicians and patients across Manhattan, specializing in weight management, diabetes, and chronic condition care.
