Services

Individualized nutrition counseling built around your goals and lifestyle

What to Expect

Every patient receives a personalized plan based on their medical history, lifestyle, and health goals. Dawn works closely with your physicians to ensure nutrition care is integrated with your overall treatment.

Approx. 60 minutes

Initial Consultation

Your first appointment is a comprehensive session where Dawn takes a complete dietary, medical, family, and weight history. She will measure you, calculate your BMI, and discuss your food preferences, daily routine, and health objectives. Together, you will develop a customized meal plan tailored to your specific needs and goals.

Approx. 30 minutes

Continuing Counseling

Follow-up appointments are scheduled on a weekly, monthly, or intermittent basis depending on your individual needs. Sessions include dietary guidance, review of your food journal, and open discussion about life circumstances that may affect your nutrition. Once you have reached your goals, Dawn will work with you to develop a long-term maintenance plan and provide ongoing support.
