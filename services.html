Our Services

Every patient gets a plan built for them

Dawn works closely with your physicians to ensure nutrition care is integrated with your overall treatment — not an afterthought.

Approx. 60 minutes

Initial Consultation

Your first appointment is a comprehensive session where Dawn takes a complete dietary, medical, family, and weight history. She measures you, calculates your BMI, and discusses your food preferences, daily routine, and health objectives. Together you develop a customized meal plan built around your specific needs and goals.

Approx. 30 minutes

Continuing Counseling

Follow-up appointments are scheduled on a weekly, monthly, or intermittent basis depending on your needs. Sessions include dietary guidance, food journal review, and discussion of life circumstances affecting your nutrition. Once you reach your goals, Dawn builds a long-term maintenance plan to help you stay on track.
