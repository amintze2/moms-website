## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Services hero
The services page SHALL display a hero section with the heading "Services".

#### Scenario: Hero renders
- **WHEN** the services page is loaded
- **THEN** a full-width hero section SHALL appear with the heading "Services"

### Requirement: Initial consultation description
The services page SHALL describe the initial consultation service.

#### Scenario: Initial consultation content displayed
- **WHEN** the services page is loaded
- **THEN** a section titled "Initial Consultation" SHALL appear describing: approximately one-hour session, complete dietary/medical/family/weight history, BMI measurement, discussion of food preferences, and creation of a customized meal plan

### Requirement: Continuing counseling description
The services page SHALL describe the continuing counseling service.

#### Scenario: Continuing counseling content displayed
- **WHEN** the services page is loaded
- **THEN** a section titled "Continuing Counseling" SHALL appear describing: approximately 30-minute follow-up appointments, weekly/monthly/intermittent scheduling, review of food journals, and maintenance plans once goals are met
