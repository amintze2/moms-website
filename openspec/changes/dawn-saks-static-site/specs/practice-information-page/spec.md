## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Practice information hero
The practice information page SHALL display a hero section with the heading "Practice Information".

#### Scenario: Hero renders
- **WHEN** the practice information page is loaded
- **THEN** a full-width hero section SHALL appear with the heading "Practice Information"

### Requirement: Practice description
The practice information page SHALL include a short paragraph describing Dawn's approach.

#### Scenario: Description text displayed
- **WHEN** the practice information page is loaded
- **THEN** a paragraph describing Dawn as a nutritionist who believes in a sensible approach to diet SHALL be visible

### Requirement: Areas of practice list
The practice information page SHALL display all areas of practice as a styled list.

#### Scenario: All practice areas listed
- **WHEN** the practice information page is loaded
- **THEN** the following areas SHALL be listed: Weight loss and management, Gastrointestinal disorders, Cholesterol and triglyceride reduction, Eating disorders and emotional eating, Wellness and diabetes management, Hypertension treatment, Pregnancy and pediatric nutrition
